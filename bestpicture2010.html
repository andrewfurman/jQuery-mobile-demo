Best Picture Nominations 2010

  1. Black Swan
  2. The Fighter
  3. The King's Speech
  4. True Grit
  5. The Social Network
  6. Inception
  7. The Kids Are Alright
  8. Toy Story 3
  9. 127 Hours
  10. Winter's Bone

Black Swan

Definitely my top choice without a doubt, didn't go into this movie with high expectations, but I was really amazed. Also, I hope Natalie Portman gets best actress. I usually don't like movies with lots of hidden symbolism, but this one had just the right amount.

The Fighter

This was another surprise for myself. It took me about 30 minutes to get into it, but then I was hooked. Great storytelling all around.

The King's Speech

Surprise hit again. I expected nothing from this movie, but I was really impressed with the engaging plot, great acting, and the historical context of the film.

True Grit

I'll admit, I basically fell asleep the first time I saw this, but I gave it another watch and it didn't disappoint, this move deserved the nomination.

The Social Network

While I did enjoy watching ZuckStar create the most magical website EVER, this movie was way to overhyped. It was basically what I expected.

Inception

Again, way too overhyped, I was kinda disappointed when I came out of the theatre because I was just expecting so much. Still dreams in dreams in dreams was a novel concept and made for lots of fun post movie jokes.

The Kids Are Alright

Even though that this isn't really my type of movie I didn't mind watching it. The plot was somewhat slow though.

Toy Story 3

It was a Toy Story movie, need I say more? If I were 5 it would be my #1 pick.

127 Hours

I know the guy lives! Thus, the movie was boring.

Winter's Bone

Same thing as Precious last year. Just really not my type of movie. I did sit through the entire movie though, so go me!
